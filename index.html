Research fellow at the Univerity of Bristol

I am a research fellow in mathematics at the University of Bristol, having recently completed my PhD at the University of Oxford, supervised by Panos Papazoglou.

My DPhil was funded by a scholarship from the Heilbronn Institute for Mathematical Research, as well as a Graduate Development Scholarship at St Anne's College, Oxford.

I previously completed the MFoCS MSc at Oxford, funded by a grant from the James Pantyfedwen Foundation. Before this, I was an undergraduate student at the University of Bristol, where I earned a BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science.

I am broadly interested in geometric group theory, particularly questions relating to splittings, coarse geometry, and algorithmic problems.

Find a CV here (last updated 13 June 2025), and feel free to get in contact for more detail.
