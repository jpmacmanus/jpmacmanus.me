Postgraduate student at the University of Oxford

I am a third year DPhil student at the Mathematical Insitute in the University of Oxford, funded by a scholarship from the Heilbronn Institute for Mathematical Research. I previously completed the MFoCS MSc here at Oxford.

Before this, I was an undergraduate student at the University of Bristol, where I earned a BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science.

I am broadly interested in combinatorial and geometric group theory, as well as complexity theory and combinatorics.

Find a short CV here, and feel free to get in contact for more detail.
